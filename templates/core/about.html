Our Story

Born in Zambia.
Built for
the World.

Kinpin Arts Media is a full-service creative and digital agency headquartered in Lusaka — delivering brand identities, digital platforms, and creative campaigns that move businesses forward.

"We started Kinpin Arts because Zambia's
businesses deserved
world-class creative."

What began as a small design studio in Lusaka has grown into one of Zambia's most trusted creative agencies — a team of strategists, designers, developers, and storytellers united by a single conviction: that great design changes businesses.

Over seven years, we've partnered with financial institutions, government bodies, sports clubs, wellness brands, and fast-growing startups — delivering work that doesn't just look good, but performs. Every project we take on is treated as a long-term investment in our client's success.

We're not a vendor. We're a creative partner — embedded in your goals, committed to your growth, and relentless in our pursuit of work that sets you apart.
